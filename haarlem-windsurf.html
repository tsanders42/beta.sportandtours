Kayak Rentals Haarlem

Fun at your own pace

  • Rent a single seater kayak and explore Haarlem at your own pace.
  • Ask us about fun places to go.
  • Up to 5 hours for only € 30.00.

Rent a kayak to explore Haarlem in a fun an relaxed way. Because kayaking is a low impact activity, it offers and accessible way to exercise and fun way to explore the waterways. We offer single-seat kayaks for rent and will provide you with possible routes to explore, raning from a leisurely 4km (1 hour) tour around the city to a scenic windmill route that takes you around the city of Haarlem on a 19,5km lap, with ample opportunities to rest and have a picknick.

You can pick up your rental kayak in the city centre, only a 5 minute walk from the market square.
