Who we are

Sport and Tours managed by Claudio Duran, who moved to The Netherlands from Chileand competed as a sup athlete in the Dutch national championships and the Sup 11 City Tour, and Paolo Iodice, who is an ASI-certified Sup instructor and brought his Italian passion for sports to the Dutch waters. We love sharing our favorite sports during our sup, hiking and biking tours in the cities of Haarlem and Amsterdam.

Join us on an adventure

Stand up paddleboard (sup):

The fast growing sport of standup paddleboarding (sup) is an excellent, easily accessible work-out and a fun way to explore the Dutch canals and rivers. Sport and Tours offers fun sup tours, helps you to learn the basics of supping or provides expert workshops to sharpen your skills.
