Who we are

Sport and Tours is managed by Claudio Duran and Paolo Iodice. We both have a strong link to outdoor activities and the water. Claudio moved to The Netherlands from Chile and competed as a sup athlete in the Dutch national championships and the Sup 11 City Tour. Paolo is a triathlete and an ASI-certified Sup instructor who brought his Italian passion for sports to the Dutch waters. We love sharing our favorite sports during our sup, hiking and biking tours in the cities of Haarlem and Amsterdam.

Join us on an adventure

Stand up paddleboard (sup):

The fast growing sport of stand up paddleboarding (sup) is an excellent, easily accessible work-out and a fun way to explore the Dutch canals and rivers. Sport and Tours offers fun sup tours in Amsterdam and Haarlem, helps you to learn the basics of supping or provides expert workshops to sharpen your skills.

Please bring a towel and clothing for swimming (bathing suite on warm days, for colder days running clothes work well.)

Hiking and walking tours:

Come join us and explore a little visited area of the Netherlands. Only a short drive from Amsterdam's bustling city center, you find true peace and quiet in the the National Park Zuid Kennemerland. Our hike will take us through this unique ecosystem where you will see traces of deers, rabits, wild cows, many birds and on lucky days a fox. Our hike will take you to the beach and you will have plenty of time for breaks (bring snacks and lunch!) while you take in the views from a dune top or soak up the sun.

The hike takes us on paved bicycle paths as well as dirt trails. Although these trails are well travelled, we will climb some sand dunes. Walking or running shoes are adviced. Hiking boots aren't required.
